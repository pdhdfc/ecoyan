About Us

We offer customers a wide range of electric motor vehicles. and Manufacturing customised services and fulfils the customer requirements.

Ecoyan is an India based, ISO 9001:2015 certified, manufacturer, supplier and service provider of electric motor vehicles. Our dedicated engineering and design team expertise in customised services and fulfils the customer requirements. It is our constant endeavour to provide personal and professional attention to each and every customer. Our vehicles are ARAI certified and Karnataka RTO certified.
At Ecoyan, we believe in small beginnings that make a big difference. We are a group of young entrepreneurs who seek to contribute to the world. Our 15 years of presence in the field of electric and hardware sector has been outstanding, and the trust we have gained enables us to venture into the new age electric motor vehicles. So, let us join hands and collectively contribute in making this planet safe and clean for the future generation. Our mission is to bring joy and delight into automobile buying and ownership.
